**Financial Analyst**

*Company:* Global Finance Corp.
*Location:* Bigcity, USA
*Type:* Full-time

**Job Description:**
Global Finance Corp. is seeking a talented Financial Analyst to join our finance team. The analyst will provide insights and support to drive financial performance and strategic decision-making.

**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct financial modeling and analysis to support business decisions.
- Prepare monthly, quarterly, and annual financial reports.
- Assist in budgeting and forecasting processes.
- Analyze market trends to identify business opportunities.
- Support senior management with ad-hoc financial analysis.

**Qualifications:**
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Accounting, or related field.
- CFA Level I certification preferred.
- 3+ years of experience in financial analysis.
- Proficient in Excel, including advanced functions and VBA.
- Knowledge of SAP or other financial systems.

**Benefits:**
- Competitive salary and bonuses
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- 401(k) plan with company match
- Paid time off and sick leave

**How to Apply:**
Interested candidates should submit their resume and cover letter to careers@globalfinance.com with the subject "Financial Analyst Position."
