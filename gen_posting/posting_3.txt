**Registered Nurse - Emergency Department**

*Company:* Mercy General Hospital
*Location:* Smallville, USA
*Type:* Full-time

**Job Description:**
Mercy General Hospital is seeking experienced Registered Nurses to join our Emergency Department team. We are committed to providing exceptional patient care in a fast-paced environment.

**Responsibilities:**
- Provide direct patient care in accordance with hospital policies and procedures.
- Assess, plan, implement, and evaluate patient care needs.
- Collaborate with interdisciplinary teams to ensure the best patient outcomes.
- Administer medications and treatments as prescribed.
- Educate patients and families on health care needs.

**Qualifications:**
- Current Registered Nurse (RN) license in the state of employment.
- Minimum of 2 years of experience in emergency or critical care nursing.
- BLS, ACLS, and PALS certifications required.
- Strong critical thinking and communication skills.
- Ability to work in a high-stress environment.

**Benefits:**
- Competitive salary and shift differentials
- Comprehensive health benefits
- Retirement savings plan
- Tuition reimbursement

Apply online at www.mercygeneralhospital.com/careers or email your resume to nursingcareers@mercygeneral.com.
