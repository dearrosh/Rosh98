**Elementary School Teacher**

*School:* Sunshine Elementary School
*Location:* Hometown, USA
*Type:* Full-time

**Job Description:**
Sunshine Elementary School is seeking a dedicated and enthusiastic Elementary School Teacher to join our educational team. The teacher will be responsible for creating a nurturing classroom environment that encourages student growth and learning.

**Responsibilities:**
- Develop and implement lesson plans that meet state curriculum standards.
- Assess student progress and adjust teaching strategies accordingly.
- Foster a positive and inclusive classroom environment.
- Communicate effectively with parents and guardians.
- Participate in professional development and school activities.

**Qualifications:**
- Bachelor's degree in Elementary Education or related field.
- Valid State Teaching Certification.
- ESL endorsement is a plus.
- Strong classroom management skills.
- Passionate about teaching and child development.

**Benefits:**
- Competitive salary based on experience and education
- Health and dental insurance
- Retirement benefits
- Professional development opportunities

**How to Apply:**
Please submit your resume, cover letter, and references to hr@sunshineelementary.edu.
